GogoGrandparent

At Health Connect, we understand that transportation can be a major barrier for older adults in accessing healthcare services. That's why we're proud to partner with GogoGrandparent to offer on-demand Uber and Lyft rides to our clients.

GogoGrandparent is a transportation service that allows seniors to book rides with Uber and Lyft using a regular phone, without the need for a smartphone or app. This makes it easy for older adults to get to their appointments, go shopping, or visit family and friends, without having to worry about the hassle of booking rides online.

To set up a ride, simply call 1 (855) 464-6872 ext 65562 and an operator will help you schedule a ride. You can choose to use Uber or Lyft, and a driver will be dispatched to your location. GogoGrandparent will send you a text or call to confirm your ride, and you can track the driver's progress in real-time.
